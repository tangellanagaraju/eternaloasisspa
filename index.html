We provide different Spa Services

Body Scrub and Wrap

Pamper your skin with our rejuvenating body scrub and wrap, designed to exfoliate and reveal a healthy, radiant glow. Our treatment gently removes dead skin cells and boosts circulation.

Couple massage

A couple massage offers a unique way for partners, friends, or even family members to unwind and reconnect. The experience allows two people to enjoy a simultaneous massage in the same room, side by side.

Pedicure service

A pedicure is a beauty treatment designed for the care and maintenance of the feet and toenails. It typically involves cleaning, exfoliating, shaping the nails, and moisturizing the feet to maintain their health and appearance.

Jaccuzzi Service

A Jacuzzi spa is a luxurious and relaxing experience that combines the soothing effects of warm water with the therapeutic benefits of massaging jets. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, muscle recovery, or a way to de-stress, a Jacuzzi spa provides an indulgent escape that rejuvenates both the body and mind.
